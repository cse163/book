Practice: Broadcasting#

What is the result of the following operation? For reference, we have shown the rules of broadcasting below.

x = np.arange(3).reshape((3, 1))
y = np.arange(3)
x + y

Rules of Broadcasting#

  • If the two arrays differ in their number of dimensions, the shape of the one with fewer dimensions is padded on itâ€™s left side.

  • If the shape of two arrays does not match on any dimension, the array with shape equal to 1 in that dimension is stretched to match the other shape. This will happen for all dimensions that donâ€™t match and one has a shape equal to 1.

  • If in any dimension the sizes disagree and neither is equal to 1, an error is raised.
